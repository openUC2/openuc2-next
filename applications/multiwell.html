High-Throughput Multiwell Screening

Fast, automated tile scans across 96- and 384-well plates with reliable autofocus, repeatable illumination, and multi-channel fluorescence. FRAME handles hands-off job execution, per-well QC, and error recovery — so your screening runs overnight without supervision.

[Composite image: 96-well plate with fluorescence channel overlays — DAPI/GFP/mCherry per well]
FRAME | 10× | 4-color | 96-well plate

Plate-to-data in one automated run

FRAME's 130×90 mm encoder-equipped XY stage covers an entire 96-well plate with absolute positional accuracy. Köhler/LED-array illumination ensures homogeneous illumination across the full field of view, eliminating per-well calibration overhead.

A hardware-triggered sCMOS camera, synchronized with the LED array and Z-focus lock, captures each well with consistent exposure across the plate. Four to five fluorescence colors can be acquired sequentially with a motorized filter-cube carousel, and a configurable overlap threshold rejects cross-talk between channels.

Key capabilities

🧪 96 / 384-well plate support

Motorized plate holders with SBS-footprint registration. Load plate, press run, come back to finished data.

💡 Homogeneous Köhler illumination

LED array and Köhler condenser deliver flat illumination across the entire field of view. Flat-field correction built into the acquisition pipeline.

🎨 4–5 color fluorescence

Motorized filter-cube carousel switches channels in <200 ms. Calibrated exposure per channel with hardware-triggered camera synchronization.

🔒 Reliable autofocus

Contrast-based autofocus per tile, with laser focus-lock fallback. Handles uneven plates and meniscus effects across the plate surface.

🤖 Job scheduler

Queue multiple plates, define acquisition recipes, and set error-recovery policies. Resume interrupted runs at the well that failed — no data loss.

📦 OMERO / OME-Zarr output

Data written directly to OMERO server or OME-Zarr archive. Per-well thumbnails and metadata for immediate QC in the browser.
