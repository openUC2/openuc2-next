7) Use Case: Space-borne Optical Engine (≤1 L, dual-color fluo + IR AF, fully autonomous)
Challenge / Requirement
≤1 L volume, ultra-low mass; only Z-focus + acquisition (XY by host robot)
Dual-color fluorescence, repeatable radiometric output, minimal drift
Through-objective IR autofocus (robust under vibration, thermal cycling, micro-g)
Fully autonomous operation (no user intervention; watchdogs; graceful recovery
Survivability: launch vibration/shock, radiation/latch-up, vacuum/outgassing, wide temp swings, connector security
Deterministic boot & mission timeline; data integrity with limited power/thermal budget
Necessary Hardware Configuration
Optical engine (inner FRAME cube): compact epi-fluorescence path, dual-color excitation (e.g., 488/635 nm) with keyed dichroic/emission filters; baffle/blackened stray-light control
IR AF module: 830 nm through-objective off-axis injection, QPD/PSD (preferred) or small CMOS at pupil-conjugate; piezo/voice-coil Z-actuator with position feedback
Objective & tube: fixed, kinematic hard-lock after calibration; rugged lens clamps; athermal spacers
Detector: small global-shutter board-CMOS, locked flange; EMI shielding; lens/tube hard-mount
Thermal/mech: conductive heat path to chassis; conformal coating; thread-locker; safety wire; locking/potted connectors; foam/metal isolators per vibe profile
Electronics: rad-tolerant or protected DC/DC; latch-up/current limiting; HW watchdog; RTC; isolated GPIO/TTL sync to host robot; low-outgassing cables
Compute: Raspberry Pi-class SBC (only if accepted by provider) or CM-form-factor; read-only root, journaling data partition; ECC-capable storage; hardware clock
Firmware/Software: finite-state machine (boot→self-test→cal→acquire→health-report); AF PID with fallback tables; radiometric self-check; telemetry & fault logs; mission script executor
Not yet good enough in FRAME
Materials/process: outgassing-rated adhesives, coatings, cable jackets; vibration-qualified mounts/connectors not finalized
Radiation/SEU strategy: latch-up protection, ECC storage, periodic scrubbing not validated
Thermal design: conductive paths and athermalization not verified across mission profile
Autonomy hardening: full watchdog/failsafe/telemetry stack and deterministic boot scripts pendin
Compactification: optical path, AF sensor, and Z-actuator integration to ≤1 L requires re-packaging and custom brackets
